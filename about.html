We Are Momentus

MOMENTUS is a platform built to connect talented photographers with people who need them. Our mission is to make it easy for freelancers to showcase their work and for clients to discover the right photographer for their special moments. Whether it’s a wedding, event, or creative project, MOMENTUS helps you find the perfect match with just a few clicks.

Our Vision

To be the go-to platform where creativity meets opportunity — empowering photographers to thrive as freelancers and helping people capture their most important memories with ease and confidence.
